Quick Insights

The Heart of a Nation

Economic Powerhouse

Contributing over 25% of Pakistan's GDP, Karachi is home to the country's stock exchange, major banks, and the two largest seaports that connect South Asia to the world.

The Great Melting Pot

Often called 'Mini Pakistan', Karachi is the only city where every linguistic and ethnic group resides in harmony, from the Sindhi and Muhajirs to the Pakhtuns and Punjabis.

Architectural Heritage

A walk through Karachi is a walk through time. Witness the Venetian-Gothic splendor of the British Raj alongside modern glass skyscrapers and historic white marble monuments.
